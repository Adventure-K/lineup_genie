Constraints

  • Active roster must be at least 9 players.
  • All lineups must have an assigned catcher or a utility player.
  • A 9-man lineup requires either one C or Util in Pos 1, and RCF must be omitted.
  • For lineups of 10 or more, each position except C, Util and DH must be assigned in Pos 1 once.
    Any remaining players must be C (1 max), DH or Util:
    • 10-man: up to 1 Util
    • 11-man: up to 2 Utils
    • 12-man: up to 3 Utils
    • 13 or more: up to 4 Utils
  • Any roster with C assigned and no Utils will generate an empty sparse table.
